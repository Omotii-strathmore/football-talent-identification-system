Football Talent Identification and Exposure System / Uhamasishaji wa Vipaji

Talanta Soka is a shared space for players, scouts, and opportunities.

The system shows players where trials and tournaments are posted, gives scouts one place to share openings, and keeps both sides on the right path with role-based access. It is built to help talent be noticed, organized, and connected in one place.

Register as Player Join as Scout
Player profiles Players create accounts, choose a role, and move into a player-specific page after signup.
Scout posts Scouts can publish trials, tournaments, and openings where players can easily find them.
Role routing After registration or login, the system sends each user to the correct area automatically.

What players get

A simple account, access to opportunities, and a clear place to begin their journey toward visibility.

What scouts get

A single posting space for trials and tournaments, plus a direct way to reach players who are ready to be noticed.

Why it matters

Talent often goes unseen because information is scattered. This system keeps the path from discovery to opportunity in one place.

How it works

1. Choose a role. 2. Create your account. 3. Get taken to the right page. 4. Explore or post opportunities.

Player path

Register as a player to see the opportunities area, then use it to follow trials and tournaments that match your goals.

Scout path

Register as a scout to share openings and guide players toward events, requirements, and next steps.
